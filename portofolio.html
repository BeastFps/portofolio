About
Me

Hi, I'm Sahil Som, a 3D Artist and Technical Artist from Meerut, India, passionate about creating high-quality game-ready assets and real-time visual experiences. I primarily work with Maya, Substance Painter, Marmoset Toolbag, and Photoshop to create detailed models, textures, and polished presentations. My work focuses on hard-surface modeling, environment assets, and clean game-art workflows while maintaining optimization and visual quality. I enjoy combining creativity with technical precision to create assets that feel immersive and production-ready. Currently, I'm building personal projects and expanding my portfolio with a strong focus on 3D modeling, texturing, and cinematic asset presentation.
